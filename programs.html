Our ICT Programs

Interactive and modern programs designed for a successful ICT career.

Diploma in Information Technology

NQF Level 6 | SAQA ID: 96861 | 3-Year Full-Time

  • 360 SAQA Credits
  • Software development, networking, and database management

    • Program Overview

    This qualification prepares students for entry-level positions in the ICT industry in either software development or communication networks. Students gain a general all-round working knowledge of the ICT industry with opportunities to specialize in second and third year.

    Admission Requirements

    • National Senior Certificate (Diploma or higher)
    • English First Additional Language (3) OR English Home Language (3)
    • Mathematics (3) OR Mathematical Literacy (5)
    • Minimum of 24 points in best six subjects
    • Limited seats based on ranking system

Extended Curriculum Program

NQF Level 6 | SAQA ID: 96862 | 4-Year Full-Time

  • 480 SAQA Credits
  • Includes foundation year for additional preparation

    • Program Overview

    This extended curriculum program includes an additional foundation year to prepare students for the diploma program. It's designed for students who demonstrate aptitude for ICT but need additional preparation to meet the mainstream program requirements.

    Admission Requirements

    • National Senior Certificate (Diploma or higher)
    • English First Additional Language (3) OR English Home Language (3)
    • Mathematics (2) OR Mathematical Literacy (3)
    • Minimum of 23 points in best six subjects
    • Selection based on aptitude test or ranking

Advanced Diploma in ICT: Applications Development

NQF Level 7 | SAQA ID: 117974 | 1-2 Years Full/Part Time

  • 120 SAQA Credits
  • Post-diploma specialization in application development

    • Program Overview

    This advanced diploma provides specialization in Applications Development, preparing students for advanced roles in the ICT industry or further postgraduate study. It incorporates the latest ICT industry requirements and focuses on continuing professional development.

    Admission Requirements

    • 360-Credit Diploma/National Diploma in ICT (NQF Level 6)
    • OR Bachelor's Degree in related IT specialization
    • OR RPL qualification evaluated to NQF level 6
    • Selection based on academic merit
    • Limited seats available
